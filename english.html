Het Scheepvaartmuseum

Bekijk in het Nederlands

Come and see one of the largest maritime collections in the world and experience 500 years of Dutch maritime history! Buy your tickets for the next day within five minutes!

The Maritime Museum started in 1916 after a successful exhibition in 1913. It moved to Amsterdam in 1973 in the countries sea warehouse. and after the renovation between 2007 and 2009 the museum has gotten completely new and improved.

Exhibitions

The Maritime Museum has four different wings: the north wing, the east wing, the south wing and the west wing. You enter through the South Wing and then you can decide in which order you go down the wings.

Westwing:
  • Amsterdam port city
  • Tale of thee whale
Northwing:
  • WIllem van de Velde & Zoon
  • Main gallery: Republic
  • Dare to Discovery | a VR journey
  • King sloop
  • VOC-ship Amsterdam
  • Steamship Christiaan Brunings
Eastwing:
  • Cartography & Curiosities
  • The Hunting Models
  • The Navigation Instruments
  • The Ship Ornaments
  • A Lost Manuscript
